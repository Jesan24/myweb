1. Digital, Creative, and Practical Education System

Objective:

To modernize the education system by integrating digital tools, creativity, and hands-on practical learning to prepare students for the 21st-century job market.

Steps:

  • Introduce smart classrooms with multimedia tools and internet access.
  • Include project-based learning and encourage critical thinking.
  • Incorporate technical, vocational, and life skills training.
  • Train teachers on modern teaching strategies.
  • Update the curriculum to focus on STEAM education.

Impact:

Builds a skilled and competitive workforce, reduces unemployment, and encourages innovation.

Development Contribution:

This project will prepare youth with modern skills, helping Bangladesh transform into a knowledge-based economy.

2. 10 Million Workplace for Unemployment

Objective:

To create 10 million job opportunities for unemployed youth in diverse fields through skill training, entrepreneurship, and digital platforms.

Steps:

  • Set up skill development centers for vocational and digital training.
  • Launch a startup incubator to support young entrepreneurs.
  • Develop an online platform to connect skilled youth with global opportunities.
  • Partner with companies to create job placements.
  • Work with government initiatives to support employment creation.

Impact:

Reduces unemployment, boosts national income, and empowers youth to become job creators.

Development Contribution:

This project will provide jobs and financial stability, contributing to economic growth and reducing poverty.

3. Islamic Education

Objective:

To promote a balanced Islamic education system that combines religious teachings with modern education, fostering moral, ethical, and intellectual growth.

Steps:

  • Set up modern Islamic schools offering religious and modern subjects.
  • Develop online platforms and apps for accessible Islamic education.
  • Train teachers on modern teaching techniques.
  • Organize workshops focusing on character building and ethics.
  • Establish research centers for higher Islamic studies.

Impact:

Nurtures ethical and morally strong individuals while promoting holistic development.

Development Contribution:

This project will create youth with strong moral values and intellectual abilities, fostering a peaceful and progressive society.

4. Digital Transportation and Space Exploration

Objective:

To modernize Bangladesh's transportation system and initiate the country's space exploration journey for national development.

Steps:

  • Introduce smart transportation systems like digital ticketing and electric vehicles.
  • Expand high-speed rail and metro systems.
  • Develop apps for efficient ridesharing and public transport tracking.
  • Establish a Bangladesh Space Research Center for training scientists.
  • Partner with international space agencies for knowledge-sharing.

Impact:

Improves connectivity, enhances productivity, and positions Bangladesh as a tech-driven country.

Development Contribution:

This project will create career opportunities in advanced technology and aerospace, helping Bangladesh compete on a global level.
