Personal Story

Early Life and Education

Md. Jesan Anjum Turzo was born on December 5, 2007, in Kishoreganj, Nilphamari. Growing up in a family shaped by his father’s job transfers, Jesan’s educational journey took him across five different schools, each offering unique learning experiences. These schools include:

  • Everest School
  • Shishu Kanon Shikkha Niketon
  • Magura Model School
  • Hazi Kasim Uddin Public School & College
  • Safiuddin Sarker Academy and College

Currently, Jesan is pursuing his education in class 11 at BAF Shaheen College Kurmitola. His dedication and hard work have been reflected in his academic achievements, earning GPA 5 in both the PSC and SSC exams.

Passion for Learning and Growth

From an early age, Jesan demonstrated a deep passion for reading, writing, and public speaking. His ability to express ideas confidently has enabled him to speak on over 10 stages, where he inspired audiences and earned numerous academic awards.

Jesan also ventured into writing and authored a book titled Read and Learn With Islam. Although the book remains unpublished, it showcases his commitment to sharing knowledge and encouraging others to learn.

Career Journey

Founding LCSSAC

In 2023, Jesan founded LCSSAC (Language Club of Safiuddin Sarker Academy & College), where he currently serves as the President. Under his leadership, the club focuses on helping students master the English language, nurturing leadership qualities, and developing future global influencers. His impactful contributions to LCSSAC reflect his strong vision and dedication.

Vision and Goals

Jesan’s long-term mission is to teach English and empower students to communicate effectively on a global scale. Through his platform English Capsule, he envisions expanding its reach globally within the next 8 years, transcending the boundaries of school-based clubs to create an international platform for learning and development.

His goals include:

  • Helping students build confidence in English communication
  • Organizing workshops, events, and innovative learning sessions
  • Growing English Capsule into a globally recognized learning platform
