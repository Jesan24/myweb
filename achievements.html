My Journey of Achievements

Public Speaking & Leadership

  • Spoken on over 10 stages, showcasing my passion and skills in public speaking.
  • Won numerous academic awards, demonstrating my dedication and excellence in education.
  • Recognized for my leadership abilities through active roles in various organizations and clubs.

Founder & Leader

  • Founder of LCSSAC (Language Club of Safiuddin Sarker Academy & College), established in 2023 with the goal of empowering students through language learning and leadership development.
  • Creator of English Capsule, an English learning initiative, where I launched my first product, an eBook titled 'English Capsule.' This platform aims to make English learning accessible globally and reach a wider audience over the next 8 years.

Collaborations & Contributions

  • Collaborated with renowned organizations, including Mujib Olympiad, Steam Olympiad, Ninish, and BioPark, to promote education and create valuable learning opportunities for students.
  • Worked closely with national media and successfully secured sponsorships for various events, including the 1st Language Festival, significantly contributing to its success.

Additional Contributions

  • Mentorship & Public Speaking: Regularly mentor students in language and public speaking, encouraging them to achieve academic and personal growth.
  • Event Organizer: Led the organization of major educational events, workshops, and festivals, positively impacting thousands of students.
  • Academic Excellence: Achieved GPA 5 in both my SSC and PSC exams, a testament to my commitment to education and personal growth.
